Predicting Water Temperature

Predicting water temperature at Ohio Street Beach in the fall of 2015, based on data collected by the city of Chicago during the summer of 2015.

Ohio Street Beach Water Temperature 2015 (Celsius)


Water Temperature Dataset

I downloaded the "Beach_Water_Quality - Automated_Sensors" dataset from the City of Chicago Data Portal. The dataset included sensor readings from several locations, but I chose to use "Ohio Street Beach" for a time series prediction, as this location had the most amount of sensor data.
